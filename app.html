BoomBap Drop: A coding Challenge

Drop the beat and let's make sweet music together!

Instructions:

Enter a number. If the number is divisible by 3, you'll make a note called "BOOM". If your number is divisible by 5, then you'll make another note, "BAP". If you so happen to make a note that is divisible by both 3 & 5 then..."BOOM-BAP", drop that track!...We got ourselves a musical contract.

Drop the beat!
